How often should I book window cleaning?

We recommend twice a year for most homes. Heavily wooded or high-traffic areas may need more frequent cleaning.

Can you clean high windows?

Yes! We’re equipped to handle multi-story homes with ladders and aerial lifts.

Is it safe?

Absolutely. We’re insured, WorkSafeBC-covered, and our crew is trained in ladder safety and rooftop work.
